Let's play Rock, Paper, Scissors!

How to Play:

You have 3 choices: Rock, Paper, or Scissors.

Play 3 rounds against the computer.

The first to get 3 points wins.

How to get a point:

  • Rock beats Scissors.
  • Paper beats Rock.
  • Scissors beats Paper.

If both choose the same, no one gets the point.
